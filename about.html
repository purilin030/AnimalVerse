About AnimalVerse

Discover. Learn. Connect with Nature.

Our Mission

AnimalVerse is an AI-enhanced animal video streaming platform designed to bring the wonders of wildlife to everyone. Our mission is to create a space where curiosity meets education — whether you're a student researching animal behaviour, a teacher looking for classroom resources, or simply someone who finds peace in watching nature unfold.

We believe that connecting people with the animal kingdom can inspire conservation awareness, support mental well-being, and spark a lifelong love for the natural world. Every video on our platform is curated to be both informative and visually captivating.

Project Overview

This project is developed as a Final Year Project (FYP) for the Bachelor of Information Systems (Honours) Information Systems Engineering at Universiti Tunku Abdul Rahman (UTAR). The platform is designed to be hosted entirely on AWS cloud infrastructure, leveraging serverless technologies for scalability and cost-effectiveness.

The current phase (FYP 1) focuses on building a feature-rich static frontend with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, deployed via AWS S3 and CloudFront for global content delivery. The video library contains curated wildlife footage across eight biological classes — from mammals and birds to amphibians and aquatic life.

Phase 2 (FYP 2) will introduce user authentication, AI-powered chatbot assistance via Amazon Lex, personalised recommendations through Amazon Bedrock, and dynamic video uploads backed by AWS Lambda and DynamoDB.

Content Library

Our video library spans 140+ animal species across 8 biological classes, with media sourced from curated collections and the YouTube Wildlife API. Each species entry includes professional footage, location data, and links to external knowledge sources for deeper exploration.

The library is organised by taxonomy — Mammals, Birds, Reptiles, Amphibians, Aquatic Life, Fish, Invertebrates, and Themed Series — making it easy to browse and discover.
